Giaraffe

The Giaraffe is a large African hoofed mammal. It is the tallest and largest living animal on Earth! Usually Giaraffes are thought of as being just one species, when in actuality there are nine sunspecies connected together!

HOW TO IDENTIFY

You may be asking yourself,How will I be able to identify this animal?
Well I am here to help you!

Physical Traits

  • Extremely long neck and legs (kinda hard to miss)
  • Spotted or geometric brown coat patterns
  • Tongue as long as two footballs
  • Short skin-covered horns
